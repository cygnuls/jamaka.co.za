Payments

We accept SnapScan payments for your convenience.

Use the SnapScan app on your smartphone to scan the QR image you see on the screen. If you don't have the app, you can download it from SnapScan's website.

Once scanned, just enter the amount due as displayed on your invoice - we'll receive confirmation of your payment once it has been processed.
