Cottage 12

This is a new chalet with room for up to five people. One room with a queen size bed and another with three single beds.

Enjoy comfort away from home with a spacious living room and dining area complete with dinner table and chairs.

A well-equipped kitchen can be used by guests to prepare meals. The porch has a table and chairs for an outdoor braai experience.

The stoep offers guests 180 degrees of scenic Cederberg views. The Rondegat river and several hiking trails are in close proximity.
