Jamaka Organic Farm & Resort lies in the heart of the Cederberg mountains. On offer are ten fully equipped self-catering cottages, camping grounds on the banks of the Rondegat river, complemented by breathtaking views, hiking trails and freshwater rock pools.

Cottages

Our cottages are specially equipped to ensure a comfortable stay. With indoor fireplaces and well furnished rooms and kitchens, you'll truly be walking into your home away from home. Not to mention verandah's from where you'll be spoiled with breathtaking views.

Visit Cottages

Campsite

The camping grounds are situated along the banks of the Rondegat River. A variety of trees and shrubs growing near the river provides umbrageous camping grounds. Hiking trails, rock pools and donkey cart rides are sure to entertain the whole family.

Visit Campsite

Farm

As the only certified organic citrus and mango farm in the Cederberg mountains, we embrace sustainable farming techniques in an effort to preserve the natural ecology whilst producing a higher quality fruit. The farm also grows organic rooibos.
